About me

I am a senior research scientist at NVIDIA, working on machine learning, speech and natural language processing. Before that, I was the TTS Team Lead at Baidu Research. I obtained my PhD in machine learning from University of California, Irvine in 2016, advised by Professor Alexander Ihler. I am passionate about building generative models for various domains, including audio, text and video.
